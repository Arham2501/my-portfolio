Hello, I'm Arham!

I'm a passionate student with a unique blend of interests that span across music and technology. My love for rhythm drives me to master the bass drums, while the melodious tones of the flute help me express my creative side.

From 2016 to 2023, I spent 8 incredible years as the bass drummer for the Shree Labdhi Vikram Jain Band (SLVJB). This experience taught me discipline, teamwork, and the power of rhythm in bringing people together. The band became my second family, and performing with them shaped my understanding of music as both an art form and a collaborative effort.

In the digital realm, I'm fascinated by artificial intelligence and its potential to revolutionize how we interact with technology. I believe that the intersection of creativity and logic can lead to incredible innovations.

When I'm not coding or practicing music, you'll find me exploring new AI technologies, composing melodies, or working on projects that combine my diverse interests.
